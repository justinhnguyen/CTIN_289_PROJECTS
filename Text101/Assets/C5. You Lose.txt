In a grim turn of events, ye choose to end Finn's life and continue yer voyage. However, fate is not on yer side, for ye soon encounter a formidable marine fleet that swiftly overpowers ye and yer crew. The marines are well aware that whoever brings an end to yer life will inherit the Captain's Curse of the Flying Dutchman, condemned to an eternal odyssey on the boundless seas. They spare yer life, and ye find yerself condemned to continue roamin' the endless waters.
